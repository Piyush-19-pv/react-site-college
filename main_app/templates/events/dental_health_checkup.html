Dental Health Checkup
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, in collaboration with a team of certified dental professionals, conducted a free Dental Health Checkup Camp for students and staff. The initiative was aimed at spreading awareness about oral hygiene and early detection of dental issues.

The event witnessed participation from over 300 individuals, who underwent screening for common dental conditions such as cavities, gum inflammation, plaque build-up, and tooth alignment problems. Students were also educated about the importance of brushing techniques, use of dental floss, and maintaining a balanced diet to prevent tooth decay.

Dentists provided personalized consultations and shared tips on how to identify symptoms of oral issues early. Free dental kits were distributed, which included a toothbrush, toothpaste, and a tongue cleaner to promote daily hygiene habits.

Special sessions were organized where students were shown visuals and videos on the long-term effects of poor dental hygiene. The college also took this opportunity to spread awareness about the adverse effects of tobacco, pan masala, and sugary drinks.
