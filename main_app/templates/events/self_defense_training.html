Self Defense Training
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
The **Self Defense Training Program** conducted at Aggarwal College Ballabgarh empowered students—especially female students—with essential skills in personal safety and situational awareness. Certified martial arts instructors guided participants through hands-on exercises in techniques like blocking, escaping holds, and controlled self-defense maneuvers.

The program also emphasized mental preparedness, confidence building, and the importance of alertness in public spaces. With high enthusiasm from students, this training became an eye-opener and a confidence booster.

The college remains committed to holistic development and safety of students through such progressive and impactful workshops.
