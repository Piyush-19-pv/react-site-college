Guest Lecture – Management Department
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
The Management Department of Aggarwal College Ballabgarh organized a dynamic Guest Lecture on “Leadership and Organizational Behavior in the Modern Era.” The session was delivered by Mr. Rakesh Bansal, a reputed business strategist and leadership coach with over 20 years of corporate experience.

The lecture focused on key leadership models, emotional intelligence, and how managers play a critical role in maintaining team morale, especially in challenging business environments. Through real-world corporate case studies, Mr. Bansal illustrated how effective communication and decision-making processes improve organizational outcomes.

Students explored the impact of cultural diversity on management styles and learned about ethical leadership, people-centric approaches, and digital disruption in management. The session was highly interactive and involved role-plays and practical scenarios to test problem-solving and critical thinking skills.

The lecture concluded with a Q&A session and motivational advice on pursuing careers in management, HR, and entrepreneurship. The department appreciated the speaker’s efforts and awarded certificates to participants, marking the event as an academic success.
