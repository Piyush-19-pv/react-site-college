National Earth Day – NSS Unit
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
On the occasion of **National Earth Day**, the NSS Unit of Aggarwal College Ballabgarh conducted a series of activities to raise awareness about environmental conservation and sustainable living. The event emphasized the importance of preserving our planet for future generations.

Volunteers planted over 200 saplings around the campus and in local parks. A rally was organized to encourage the use of eco-friendly practices such as reducing plastic use, conserving water, and supporting renewable energy. Faculty members, students, and local residents enthusiastically participated in the event.

The college also hosted a poster-making competition and eco-quiz, focusing on biodiversity, climate change, and green technology. Winners were felicitated with certificates and eco-friendly gifts.

Through these impactful initiatives, the NSS Unit reinforced the message: "Every day is Earth Day." The event concluded with a pledge to promote green living and make our surroundings cleaner and healthier.
