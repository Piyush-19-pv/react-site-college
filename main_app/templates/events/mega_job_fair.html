Mega Job Fair
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh successfully hosted a **Mega Job Fair** bringing together top national and regional companies under one roof. The event aimed to bridge the gap between academia and industry by offering students real-time placement opportunities.

More than 35 companies including Infosys, Wipro, TCS, Genpact, and startups participated, offering roles in IT, HR, Sales, Finance, and Operations. Over 800 students from multiple institutions attended, and over 200 candidates were shortlisted on the spot.
