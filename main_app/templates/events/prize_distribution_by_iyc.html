Prize Distribution by IYC
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
The Institutional Youth Committee (IYC) of Aggarwal College Ballabgarh organized a grand **Prize Distribution Ceremony** to celebrate the exceptional achievements of students in academics, sports, and co-curricular activities. The event aimed to boost morale and honor the hard work of the youth.

Students who excelled in inter-college competitions, internal quizzes, and social outreach programs were awarded trophies and certificates. Chief guests included dignitaries from the education sector and alumni of the college.

The IYC emphasized youth empowerment and encouraged students to stay active in both academic and social development. The function concluded with a motivational address and group photos with award winners.
