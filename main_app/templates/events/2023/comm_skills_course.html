Communication Skills Course

Offered by Aggarwal College

Communication is the backbone of personal and professional success, and to empower students with these essential skills, Aggarwal College has introduced a specialized **Communication Skills Course**. This course aims to improve the speaking, listening, writing, and non-verbal communication abilities of students to make them industry-ready and socially confident.

The course curriculum is designed in collaboration with communication experts and industry professionals. It includes modules on verbal articulation, group discussions, presentations, resume writing, body language, and cross-cultural communication. Each topic is taught through interactive lectures, activities, role-plays, and live demonstrations.
