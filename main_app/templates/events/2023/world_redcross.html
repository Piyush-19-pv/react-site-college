World Red Cross Day

Organized by Aggarwal College

**World Red Cross Day** was celebrated with immense enthusiasm at Aggarwal College to honor the humanitarian efforts and selfless service of Red Cross volunteers across the globe. The event aimed to instill values of compassion, service, and global citizenship among students.

The celebration began with a formal inauguration where the principal highlighted the significance of the Red Cross Movement and the role it plays in disaster relief, medical aid, and humanitarian outreach. The event was attended by faculty, NSS volunteers, and YRC unit members.

A key highlight of the day was a guest talk by a certified Red Cross trainer who shared inspiring stories of fieldwork and discussed the importance of emergency response skills, first aid training, and blood donation. Students were deeply engaged and asked several thoughtful questions.
