The Department of Chemistry at Aggarwal College organized an insightful guest lecture aimed at enhancing students' academic exposure and understanding of real-world applications in the field of chemistry. Overall, the guest lecture served as a powerful academic enrichment activity that bridged the gap between classroom learning and practical science. The Department expressed gratitude for the enriching session and encouraged students to take part in more such academic forums in the future.
