Youth Dialogue

Organized by Aggarwal College

**Youth Dialogue**, hosted at Aggarwal College, was a vibrant platform that empowered young minds to express their ideas, challenge societal issues, and envision a better future. The event focused on engaging students in meaningful discussions, encouraging leadership, and promoting civic participation.

The program started with a welcome address highlighting the theme "Youth Voice for Nation Building". It emphasized how youth participation is crucial for addressing contemporary issues like education, environment, gender equality, and innovation. A motivational video set the tone for the sessions ahead.

The dialogue included a series of moderated panel discussions where students expressed their thoughts on diverse topics including the role of youth in policy-making, digital ethics, and sustainable development. Each panel was followed by open-floor interaction, giving every participant a chance to be heard.

Special guest speakers from academic and social sectors shared their journeys and emphasized the role of active listening, empathy, and constructive debate. They encouraged students to voice opinions responsibly and remain informed citizens in a democratic society.

All participants were encouraged to take the Youth Responsibility Pledge for creating a progressive and inclusive society.
