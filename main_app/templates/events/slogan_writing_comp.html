Slogan Writing Competition
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
The **Slogan Writing Competition** was organized at Aggarwal College to encourage creative expression among students. The competition aimed to raise awareness on social issues such as environment, gender equality, health, and patriotism.

Students from various streams participated with immense enthusiasm, displaying impactful, witty, and thought-provoking slogans. The event was judged based on originality, relevance, creativity, and clarity.

The initiative helped students polish their language skills and provided them a platform to voice their opinions through powerful words. The winning slogans were displayed across the campus and received widespread appreciation.
