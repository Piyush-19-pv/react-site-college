One Day MBA Expo

The One Day MBA Expo was successfully conducted by the Department of Management, bringing together professionals, alumni, and students to explore opportunities in the field of business administration.

The event featured stalls, expert talks, career guidance sessions, and interactive displays highlighting various specializations in MBA such as Finance, HR, Marketing, and Entrepreneurship. Attendees had the opportunity to network and gain industry insights.

The Expo aimed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world corporate practices. It served as a dynamic platform for students aspiring to pursue careers in management.
