National Legal Services Day

The **National Legal Services Day** was observed to spread awareness about the provisions of free legal aid to the weaker sections of society and to ensure justice for all. The event aimed at educating students and the general public about the rights and duties enshrined in the Constitution.

Eminent legal professionals and faculty members addressed the gathering, highlighting the significance of legal awareness, the role of the judiciary, and how citizens can access legal services through proper channels.

The session concluded with a Q&A round where participants interacted with the speakers. Informative brochures were also distributed during the event.
