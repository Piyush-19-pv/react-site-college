Guest Lecture by English Department

The Department of English organized a Guest Lecture on “Language, Literature, and Identity” to enrich students’ understanding of the dynamic relationship between language and social narratives. The session was conducted by Dr. Kavita Bansal, a literature scholar from Delhi University.

The lecture explored postcolonial perspectives, the impact of regional languages, and literary analysis techniques. Students actively participated in discussions and presented thoughtful questions.

The session encouraged critical thinking and inspired students to delve deeper into literary theory and interdisciplinary research. It proved to be an intellectually stimulating experience for all participants.
