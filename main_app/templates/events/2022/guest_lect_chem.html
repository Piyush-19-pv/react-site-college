Guest Lecture by Chemistry Department

The Department of Chemistry at Aggarwal College organized an insightful Guest Lecture on recent advancements in chemical sciences. The session was led by Dr. Neelam Sharma, a renowned researcher and academician.

Students learned about cutting-edge research in organic synthesis, green chemistry, and sustainable solutions for the environment. Dr. Sharma emphasized the role of chemistry in everyday life and encouraged students to explore research careers.

The event concluded with a vibrant Q&A session, certificates of participation, and positive feedback from both students and faculty. It served as a valuable addition to the academic enrichment of science students.
