Environment Protection Drive

Aggarwal College initiated a powerful Environment Protection Drive to spread awareness about conservation practices, pollution control, and sustainability. The event brought together students, staff, and volunteers to pledge action for safeguarding nature.

Activities included poster making, a green rally, tree plantation, and a panel discussion on renewable energy. Distinguished speakers from the Forest Department emphasized the importance of collective responsibility toward reducing environmental degradation.

The event inspired participants to take actionable steps like reducing plastic use, saving energy, and promoting biodiversity.
