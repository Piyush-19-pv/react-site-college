Career Counselling Session

Aggarwal College organized a detailed Career Counselling Session aimed at helping students explore diverse career paths and understand modern job market trends. The event featured experienced counselors, psychologists, and education consultants who addressed both academic and emotional aspects of career planning.

The experts guided students on stream selection, skill assessment, goal setting, and how to choose a career aligned with their personality and interests. One-on-one counselling booths were also arranged for personalized advice.

The session proved to be a major success, enhancing the awareness and confidence levels among students. Participation certificates were provided at the end of the session.
