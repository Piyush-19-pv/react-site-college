Career Awareness Programme

Aggarwal College organized an insightful Career Awareness Programme to guide students towards meaningful career paths and help them align their academic journey with future aspirations. The event was conducted in collaboration with experienced career counselors and industry professionals.

The session included expert talks, skill development strategies, resume building tips, and interactive Q&A segments. Students received clarity on diverse fields like civil services, corporate jobs, higher education, startups, and creative industries.

The event motivated students to plan proactively and provided valuable resources for career planning. Certificates of participation were distributed at the end of the session.
