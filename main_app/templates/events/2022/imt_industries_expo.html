IMT Industries Expo Visit

A group of students from our college visited the IMT Industries Expo to gain real-world exposure to industrial innovations, machinery, and manufacturing trends. The expo was held at the Industrial Model Township (IMT), bringing together national and international industrial participants.

Students interacted with experts from sectors like automation, robotics, packaging, electrical components, mechanical tools, and green energy solutions. This event served as a bridge between academic learning and practical industrial applications.

Faculty mentors guided the students, encouraging them to explore career opportunities and startup ideas within the industrial domain.
