Environment Awareness Campaign

To promote the significance of sustainable living and ecological responsibility, Aggarwal College organized a vibrant Environment Awareness Campaign. The event aimed to sensitize students about pressing environmental issues and encouraged them to adopt eco-friendly habits.

The activities included a green pledge, sapling distribution, slogan writing, and an awareness rally conducted by NSS and YRC volunteers. Keynote speakers from the Department of Environmental Studies highlighted practical steps for reducing carbon footprints and protecting biodiversity.

This initiative motivated youth to take ownership in building a greener and cleaner future for coming generations.
