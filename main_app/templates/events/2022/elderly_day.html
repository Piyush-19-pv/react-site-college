International Elderly Day Celebration

On the occasion of International Day of Older Persons, Aggarwal College paid heartfelt tribute to the elderly community, acknowledging their wisdom, contributions, and sacrifices.

The event was organized by NSS volunteers and featured interactive sessions with senior citizens, cultural performances by students, and a special health awareness talk by medical experts focusing on geriatric care and wellness.

It was an emotional and enlightening program that reminded students to show gratitude and respect towards the older generation.
