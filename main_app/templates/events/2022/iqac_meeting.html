IQAC Meeting

The IQAC Meeting (Internal Quality Assurance Cell) was conducted to assess and enhance the academic and administrative performance of the institution. Faculty members and coordinators from various departments actively participated in the meeting.

Key points discussed included curriculum updates, outcome-based education, feedback mechanisms, research output, and innovative teaching practices. Emphasis was placed on enhancing the quality culture and continuous improvement in institutional practices.

The meeting concluded with an action plan for the upcoming academic year, with clear goals for quality benchmarks and student-centric approaches. The collaborative spirit and data-driven decisions were appreciated by all stakeholders.
