Children's Day Celebration

Aggarwal College celebrated Children's Day with great enthusiasm and joy, commemorating the birth anniversary of Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru, who loved children dearly. The event aimed to foster joy, creativity, and togetherness among the students.

A variety of activities were organized, including singing, dancing, storytelling, drawing competitions, and interactive games. Teachers also put on cultural performances to entertain and encourage the students.

The day concluded with refreshments and small gifts distributed to the participants. The celebration served as a beautiful reminder of the importance of childhood and the responsibility to protect and nurture young minds.
