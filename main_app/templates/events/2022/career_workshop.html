A Workshop on Career Development

Aggarwal College organized a comprehensive Workshop on Career Development to empower students with insights and strategies essential for successful career building. The event aimed to bridge the gap between academics and industry readiness.

The workshop covered key topics like resume building, interview preparation, effective communication, digital presence, and entrepreneurship. Renowned career mentors and industry experts engaged with students through interactive sessions and activities.

The practical exposure and motivational guidance during the workshop helped students discover their strengths and align their goals accordingly. Feedback from students indicated high satisfaction and enthusiasm.
