Induction Program

The Induction Program for new students was conducted with great enthusiasm to welcome them into the academic journey at our institution. It was designed to help students adapt to the college environment, understand institutional values, and build a sense of belonging.

The program included sessions on academic rules, examination systems, library resources, anti-ragging policies, value education, and career guidance. Motivational talks by the Principal and experienced faculty members encouraged students to pursue excellence in academics and extracurriculars.

Ice-breaking sessions, orientation activities, and cultural performances added vibrance and helped students build connections with peers and mentors.
