World AIDS Day Awareness Program

On the occasion of World AIDS Day, Aggarwal College organized an awareness campaign to educate students and staff about HIV/AIDS. The program emphasized the importance of early detection, regular testing, and the need to eliminate stigma surrounding the disease.

Health experts shared vital insights on prevention methods and the latest treatments available. Students also took part in slogan writing and poster making activities to show their solidarity with people living with HIV/AIDS.

The principal encouraged youth to become ambassadors of change and promote safe, informed choices in their communities. The event concluded with a pledge to support awareness efforts and stand against discrimination.
