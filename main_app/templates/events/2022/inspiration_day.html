Inspiration Day

Inspiration Day was celebrated with great enthusiasm to ignite motivation, self-confidence, and positivity among students. The day featured stories of success, courage, innovation, and social change shared by special guest speakers and faculty members.

Eminent personalities from different fields were invited to deliver speeches that encouraged students to overcome challenges and strive for excellence. Real-life stories of struggle and perseverance created a strong emotional and motivational impact.

Cultural programs, student-led presentations, and a “Wall of Inspiration” activity were also part of the event. The session concluded with a pledge to lead a life filled with values, purpose, and ambition.
