Blockchain Seminar 2023

Aggarwal College recently organized an enlightening Blockchain Seminar aimed at educating students on the future of decentralized technology. Industry experts from the blockchain sector were invited to share their knowledge and real-world insights.

The session covered topics such as cryptocurrencies, smart contracts, NFTs, and how blockchain is revolutionizing finance, education, and governance. Students also got a chance to participate in a live Q&A with speakers.

The seminar proved to be highly interactive and informative, providing students with a strong foundation in one of the fastest-growing fields in technology. Certificates were distributed to all attendees.
