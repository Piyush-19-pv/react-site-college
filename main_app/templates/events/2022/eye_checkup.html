Eye Checkup Camp

Aggarwal College organized a free Eye Checkup Camp in collaboration with reputed ophthalmologists and health professionals. The aim was to promote vision awareness and offer accessible eye care services to students and staff.

During the event, participants underwent visual acuity tests, eye pressure checks, and counseling on common eye problems like dry eyes, screen strain, and refractive errors. Eyeglasses were recommended where necessary.

The initiative was highly appreciated and helped many identify unnoticed issues. The college plans to conduct more health camps regularly.
