Natak by Commerce Students

The Commerce students of Aggarwal College organized a powerful and engaging street play (Natak) on a socially relevant theme. The performance aimed to spread awareness and provoke thought among viewers through strong dialogue and creative expression.

The event was well received by the audience, with students showcasing their talent in acting, scriptwriting, and coordination. The Natak brought attention to real-world issues through humor, drama, and emotion.

The objective of this activity was to encourage students to use art as a medium of change, while building teamwork, creativity, and public speaking skills. It was performed in the college campus courtyard and was applauded by all present.
