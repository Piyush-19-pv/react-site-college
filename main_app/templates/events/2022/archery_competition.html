Archery Competition 2023

Aggarwal College recently hosted an exhilarating Archery Competition on campus grounds as part of its annual sports week. The event witnessed active participation from both male and female students representing various departments.

Participants displayed exceptional focus, discipline, and competitive spirit as they aimed for the bullseye. The competition was conducted under the supervision of professional archery coaches, ensuring fairness and adherence to official rules.

Winners were awarded medals and certificates by the Principal, who appreciated their commitment and talent. The event inspired many students to explore archery as a skill-building and recreational activity.
