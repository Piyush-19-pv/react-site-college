FDP on Holistic Wellness

Aggarwal College organized a Faculty Development Program (FDP) on Holistic Wellness focusing on physical, mental, and emotional well-being of educators. The program covered mindfulness practices, yoga sessions, time management, and stress-relieving techniques.

Experts from the health and wellness industry shared scientifically-backed strategies to help faculty maintain balance and build emotional resilience. The event saw active participation from departments across the college.

The FDP was a refreshing break from routine, leaving faculty members feeling rejuvenated and equipped with tools to bring wellness into their professional lives.
