Navy Day Celebration

Navy Day was observed at Aggarwal College to honor the valor, commitment, and achievements of the Indian Navy. The event served as an opportunity for students to understand the significance of maritime security and to appreciate the role played by the Indian Navy in protecting the nation's interests.

Students delivered informative presentations, speeches, and a short cultural performance themed around naval courage and discipline. The session also included a visual presentation on Indian Navy's operations and milestones.

The program concluded with a pledge of patriotism and awareness about career opportunities in the Indian Navy, leaving the participants inspired and better informed.
