Activities by NSS

The NSS unit of Aggarwal College has consistently engaged students in nation-building activities. With a vision of community service and personality development, various events were conducted throughout the year.

These included cleanliness drives, plantation programs, awareness campaigns on health and hygiene, blood donation camps, and visits to villages under the "Adopt a Village" initiative. Students participated enthusiastically, learning the values of discipline, teamwork, and social responsibility.

NSS remains an integral part of student life, helping young minds grow into responsible citizens dedicated to community welfare.
