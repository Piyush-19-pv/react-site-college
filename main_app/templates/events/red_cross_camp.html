Red Cross Camp
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
The **Red Cross Camp** organized at Aggarwal College Ballabgarh aimed at creating awareness about the importance of voluntary blood donation, health safety, and humanitarian service. Conducted in collaboration with health authorities and Red Cross officials, the camp included check-ups, counseling, and awareness lectures.

Students actively participated in sessions on first aid, emergency response, and the role of youth in disaster relief. Blood donation drives and wellness booths were also set up during the event.
