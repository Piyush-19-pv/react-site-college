Guest Lecture – Computer Science Department
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
The Computer Science Department at Aggarwal College hosted an engaging Guest Lecture on “Artificial Intelligence and Emerging Technologies” by Dr. Radhika Mehra, a prominent AI researcher and industry consultant.

She discussed the rapid advancements in machine learning, neural networks, and robotics. Students were introduced to real-world use cases of AI in fields such as healthcare, transportation, education, and e-governance. The speaker emphasized the importance of ethics in AI and the future role of quantum computing.

A live demo of an AI chatbot and predictive analytics model was conducted, keeping the session interactive and application-driven. Students showed immense curiosity and asked questions about careers in AI, data science, and cybersecurity.

The lecture concluded with encouragement to explore research, open-source projects, and continuous upskilling. The department extended a warm thanks and presented a certificate of appreciation to the guest. All attendees received participation certificates.
