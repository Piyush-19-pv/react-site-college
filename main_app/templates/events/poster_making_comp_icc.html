Poster Making Competition – ICC
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
To raise awareness on issues related to **gender sensitivity and women's empowerment**, the Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) of Aggarwal College Ballabgarh organized a **Poster Making Competition** for students across various departments.

The event provided a platform for students to showcase their creativity while conveying powerful messages about equality, safety, and respect. Over 50 students participated, and their posters were evaluated on artistic creativity, message clarity, and impact.

The initiative successfully encouraged students to express their thoughts on critical topics like harassment prevention, inclusivity, and mental health. The selected posters were displayed on campus to further spread the messages among peers.

The college faculty and ICC members appreciated the enthusiasm and talent displayed by participants, reinforcing the message that **awareness starts with expression**.
