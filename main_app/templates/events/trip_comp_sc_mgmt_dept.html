Trip – Comp Sc & Mgmt Dept
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
The **Computer Science and Management Departments** of Aggarwal College jointly organized an educational trip aimed at combining academic exposure with cultural enrichment. The trip included visits to tech companies, management institutions, and historical sites that aligned with the subjects and aspirations of both departments.

The trip itinerary included destinations known for innovation and entrepreneurship. Students visited a software development firm where they interacted with industry professionals who shared insights on real-world software development life cycles, emerging technologies like AI/ML, and startup journeys. The management students explored an incubation centre and engaged in live business case discussions.

Apart from academic exposure, the students enjoyed team-building activities, cultural programs, and sightseeing to create a wholesome experience. Key locations included city monuments, innovation parks, and tech zones. Faculty ensured discipline and safety throughout while fostering free interaction and experiential learning.

This initiative was highly successful in bridging classroom learning with practical understanding. Students expressed joy and satisfaction, describing the trip as both educational and refreshing. The memories and knowledge gained will undoubtedly help shape their academic and professional growth. Such trips are now being planned annually for continuous engagement.
