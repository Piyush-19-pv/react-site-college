IQAC Meeting

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of Aggarwal College organized a strategic meeting to review ongoing quality initiatives and academic processes. The meeting aimed to strengthen the internal systems and enhance institutional performance in accordance with NAAC standards.

Key stakeholders including faculty members, department heads, and administrative officers participated in the discussion. Topics such as outcome-based education, feedback mechanisms, and best practices for accreditation were thoroughly examined.

The meeting concluded with actionable insights, future planning, and policy formation for sustainable academic excellence.
