Interzonal Fest

The Interzonal Fest at Aggarwal College is a grand celebration of talent, teamwork, and creativity. Bringing together students from different colleges and zones, this fest serves as a platform to showcase skills in academics, sports, arts, and cultural expressions.

With vibrant competitions, energetic performances, and engaging exhibitions, the fest promotes a healthy spirit of competition and cultural exchange. Events included debates, dance battles, quizzes, drama, painting, and tech contests.

Faculty and guest judges from reputed institutions graced the event. Winners were awarded certificates and trophies, and participants carried home unforgettable memories and experiences.

The Interzonal Fest truly reflected the dynamic energy and enthusiasm of youth, celebrating diversity and talent across institutions.
