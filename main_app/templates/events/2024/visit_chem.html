Visit to Chemistry Department

Students from Aggarwal College participated in a guided visit to the Chemistry Department, exploring various experiments, instruments, and real-world chemical applications. The session offered insights into chemical safety protocols and modern laboratory practices.

Experts explained key processes and students were encouraged to interact and ask questions, fostering scientific curiosity and critical thinking. The visit also included demonstrations of organic reactions and spectroscopy equipment.

The experience helped bridge theoretical knowledge with practical applications, and inspired many students to consider careers in the chemical sciences.
