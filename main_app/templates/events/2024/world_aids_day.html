World AIDS Day Awareness Program

Aggarwal College organized a special event on World AIDS Day to raise awareness about HIV/AIDS and break the stigma associated with the disease. The program emphasized the importance of education, compassion, and early detection.

Medical experts delivered informative talks about transmission, prevention methods, and the latest advancements in treatment. Students showcased posters, red ribbon campaigns, and street plays to engage the campus community.

The event served as a powerful reminder of the global commitment required to eradicate HIV/AIDS and support those affected.
