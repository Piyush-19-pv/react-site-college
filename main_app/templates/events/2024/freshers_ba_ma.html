Aggarwal College Ballabgarh extended a grand welcome to the new batches of BA and MA students through an energetic and colorful Freshers Party. The event aimed to help newcomers ease into college life and get acquainted with seniors, faculty, and campus culture.

The celebration was filled with music, dance, laughter, and lively interactions. The college auditorium was decorated with vibrant themes and a stage was set for performances that showcased the diverse talents of the students.

Highlights of the event included solo and group dances, songs, poetry, and a fashion walk. The energy was infectious as students from both UG and PG courses enthusiastically participated and cheered each other on.

Titles such as Mr. and Miss Fresher, Best Smile, and Star Performer were awarded after rounds of talent, Q&A, and ramp walk. The winners were honored with sashes and trophies, adding sparkle to their first day at college.

Faculty members shared encouraging messages, highlighting the importance of academics, discipline, and co-curricular activities. Principal Dr. Seema Dahiya welcomed all newcomers and expressed hope for a fruitful academic journey ahead.

The event concluded with group photographs and refreshments, leaving everyone with a smile and memorable experiences. The event successfully fostered a sense of belonging and excitement among the new students.
