Communication Engineering

Aggarwal College hosted an enlightening seminar on Communication Engineering, designed to familiarize students with the latest trends in telecommunication, wireless networking, and digital signal processing. This technical session was specially curated for engineering aspirants and B.Voc/IT students who have a keen interest in electronics and communication.

The seminar focused on real-world applications of 5G technology, IoT communication, satellite systems, and embedded systems that define the future of global connectivity. Students learned how signal transmission, modulation, and data encryption are used across industries such as defense, space research, and smart cities.

The guest speaker, an industry expert from BSNL, provided practical demonstrations and insights into core telecom hardware, antenna theory, microwave engineering, and fiber optics. The event also included a Q&A round, where students got answers to their doubts about career opportunities in communication tech fields.

This session was a perfect blend of knowledge and curiosity, empowering students to think innovatively and push boundaries in the world of modern communication.
