Archery Tournament

Aggarwal College Ballabgarh recently organized a thrilling **Archery Tournament** to promote physical fitness, concentration, and traditional sports among students. The event witnessed enthusiastic participation from different departments, showcasing not just athletic skill but also discipline and precision.
