Freshers Party

The Freshers Party at Aggarwal College, Ballabgarh, was an unforgettable affair, full of vibrant energy and excitement. Organized by the senior students and faculty, it welcomed the newcomers to the campus in a lively, fun-filled manner.

The event began with a warm welcome speech and introductions, followed by cultural performances like solo singing, dance battles, poetry, and skits. The audience cheered as each act brought laughter, joy, and applause.

A highlight of the celebration was the Mr. and Miss Fresher contest, where students showcased their talents, confidence, and creativity. Winners were crowned with titles, sashes, and trophies.

Faculty members joined the event and addressed the students with motivating words, emphasizing friendship, discipline, and the opportunities available at the college.

Colorful decorations, photo booths, music, and refreshments created a festive atmosphere. The event concluded with a DJ dance session where students enjoyed the beat and mingled freely, marking the beginning of new friendships and memories.
