Environmental Control Day

Aggarwal College, Ballabgarh observed **Environmental Control Day** to highlight the importance of sustainable living and climate-conscious decisions. The initiative was aimed at raising awareness among students about environmental degradation and equipping them with knowledge on how to make a positive impact on the planet.

The event featured a series of lectures, workshops, and hands-on activities like plantation drives, waste management demonstrations, and pollution control presentations. Students actively participated in designing posters, banners, and models promoting green practices.

Eminent environmentalists and faculty members discussed practical ways to reduce one’s carbon footprint, conserve water, and adopt a zero-waste lifestyle. The discussion also emphasized India's environmental policies, laws, and the role of youth in achieving the country’s green goals.

A major highlight was a short film screening that portrayed real-life environmental issues and solutions, followed by an open discussion. Certificates were awarded to participants who showcased innovative ideas and commitment to environmental responsibility.

The program concluded with a pledge ceremony where students vowed to reduce plastic usage, plant more trees, and spread awareness in their communities. Principal Dr. XYZ applauded the collective enthusiasm and stressed the importance of education in environmental reform.
