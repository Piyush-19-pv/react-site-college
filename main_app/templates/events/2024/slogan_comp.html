Slogan Writing Competition

The Slogan Writing Competition organized by Aggarwal College aimed to encourage creativity and awareness on contemporary social issues. Participants expressed powerful messages through impactful and original slogans.

The competition witnessed active participation from students across different departments. Topics included themes like Environmental Conservation, Women Empowerment, Drug Awareness, and National Unity.

Winning entries were selected based on originality, relevance, and creativity. This event provided students with a platform to reflect their thoughts and social concerns through crisp and catchy lines.
