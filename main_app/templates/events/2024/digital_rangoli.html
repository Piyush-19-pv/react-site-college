Digital Rangoli

The Department of Computer Science at Aggarwal College, Ballabgarh organized a vibrant and creative **Digital Rangoli Competition** aimed at combining traditional Indian art with digital design. This innovative event encouraged students to use digital tools to express cultural creativity.

Participants crafted mesmerizing rangoli patterns using graphic design software, illustrating a perfect blend of art and technology. This not only allowed them to showcase artistic skills but also enhanced their digital literacy.

A panel of faculty judged the entries based on creativity, color combination, symmetry, and thematic presentation. The event received overwhelming participation from students across departments and promoted a unique way of celebrating Indian traditions in a modern format.

The winners were awarded certificates and appreciation for their talent and innovation.
