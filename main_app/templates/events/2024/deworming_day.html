Deworming Day

Aggarwal College, Ballabgarh, conducted **Deworming Day** to promote the health and well-being of students by preventing parasitic worm infections. This national initiative aligns with the governmentâ€™s vision under the National Deworming Program.

As part of the awareness campaign, faculty and volunteers educated students about the importance of deworming, its schedule, side effects, and preventive practices like washing hands, using clean water, and maintaining hygiene.

Medical professionals administered deworming tablets (Albendazole) under proper guidance, especially to children and adolescents from surrounding areas and college students. Awareness posters and digital displays were used to spread information across the campus.

The effort was praised by both students and staff as a major step toward a healthy and infection-free student community.
