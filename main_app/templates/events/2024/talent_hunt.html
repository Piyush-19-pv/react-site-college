Talent Hunt Program

The Talent Hunt program at Aggarwal College was organized to discover and showcase the diverse creative abilities of our students. The event saw enthusiastic participation in singing, dancing, mimicry, poetry, art, and drama.

It was a vibrant celebration of young talent, encouraging students to step forward and express their unique gifts on stage. Judges from various departments rated performances, and winners were honored with certificates and prizes.

The event not only fostered a spirit of healthy competition but also helped build self-confidence, collaboration, and cultural enrichment among participants.
