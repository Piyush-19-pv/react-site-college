Guest Lecture – Commerce Department

The Department of Commerce at Aggarwal College, Ballabgarh, hosted a Guest Lecture on the topic “Modern Trends in Business and Commerce” on campus. The session aimed to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world commerce practices.

The guest speaker, Mr. Arvind Kumar, a senior Chartered Accountant and business consultant, enlightened students about evolving corporate finance strategies, GST applications, and startup ecosystem insights. His talk was interactive and filled with practical examples.

Students participated actively and raised thoughtful questions regarding taxation, e-commerce, and career options in commerce. The guest shared his own experiences working in the corporate sector and guided students about future opportunities in the financial domain.

The lecture was well-received by students and faculty alike. Dr. Neetu Sharma, Head of Commerce Dept, delivered a vote of thanks and emphasized the importance of practical learning for commerce students.
