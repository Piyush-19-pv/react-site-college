Drug Awareness Seminar

Aggarwal College, Ballabgarh hosted a thought-provoking **Drug Awareness Seminar** aimed at educating students about the rising challenges related to substance abuse. The seminar was organized in collaboration with local health officials and NGOs, and witnessed active participation from students and faculty alike.

Expert speakers highlighted the physiological, psychological, and legal consequences of drug addiction. Real-life stories, visuals, and interactive Q&A sessions were included to make the session impactful and relatable.

The goal of the seminar was to empower students with knowledge and make them aware of the dangers of narcotic usage, while also spreading awareness about the support systems and helplines available to those in need.

Such events not only raise consciousness but also help in fostering a supportive, drug-free college environment. All attendees received participation certificates and awareness pamphlets.
