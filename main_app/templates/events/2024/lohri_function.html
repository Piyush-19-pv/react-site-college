Lohri Function

Aggarwal College celebrated the vibrant festival of Lohri with traditional zeal and cultural fervor. The event began with a bonfire lighting ceremony followed by folk dances like Bhangra and Gidda performed by enthusiastic students.

Faculty members joined the celebration and shared the significance of Lohri, a festival that marks the end of winter and the harvesting of crops in Punjab. Traditional songs and sweets like rewari, popcorn, and peanuts added to the joyous vibe.

The cultural committee beautifully decorated the venue with Punjabi motifs, and the spirit of togetherness and tradition echoed throughout the evening.
