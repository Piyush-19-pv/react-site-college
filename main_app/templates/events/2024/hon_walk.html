Honour Walk

Aggarwal College organized a special Honour Walk to celebrate the remarkable achievements of its students, faculty, and alumni who have brought pride to the institution.

The walk was a ceremonial procession across the campus featuring flag bearers, applause lines, and speeches. Honorees were awarded certificates and commemorative mementos. It served as a strong motivation for peers and juniors.

The event emphasized the values of excellence, commitment, and discipline. Parents and dignitaries from various academic and civil sectors were invited to witness this proud moment.

It concluded with a group photo and refreshments for the honorees and guests.
