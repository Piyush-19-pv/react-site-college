Poster Making Competition
Computer Science Dept.

The Computer Science Department of Aggarwal College organized a vibrant Poster Making Competition focused on technology, AI, and digital transformation. The event encouraged students to creatively express their understanding of modern computing trends and cyber awareness through visuals.

Over 50 students from various semesters participated and showcased their innovative ideas on topics like cyber security, cloud computing, data privacy, and smart cities. Judges from the CS faculty evaluated entries based on creativity, clarity, and relevance to the theme.

Winners were awarded certificates and digital gift cards. The event successfully promoted digital literacy and artistic skills among tech-savvy students.
