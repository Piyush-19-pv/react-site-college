Voter Awareness Campaign

Aggarwal College organized a Voter Awareness Campaign to promote active and informed participation in the democratic process. The campaign aimed to educate students and the community about the significance of voting in shaping the nation.

Through speeches, skits, posters, and interactive sessions, students learned about their voting rights, eligibility criteria, and the procedure to register as voters. The event highlighted the role of youth in making democracy stronger.

The campaign concluded with an oath-taking ceremony where students pledged to vote responsibly and encourage others to do the same.
