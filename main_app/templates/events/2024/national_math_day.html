National Mathematics Day

Aggarwal College celebrated National Mathematics Day with great enthusiasm to honor the birth anniversary of the legendary Indian mathematician, Srinivasa Ramanujan. The celebration aimed to raise awareness about the importance of mathematics in day-to-day life and inspire young minds to explore the subject creatively.

Various inter-departmental activities like quiz competitions, poster making, mental math races, and puzzles were organized.
