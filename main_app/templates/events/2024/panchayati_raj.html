Panchayati Raj Day

On the occasion of Panchayati Raj Day, Aggarwal College organized an educational and awareness program to highlight the significance of local self-governance in India. The event shed light on the role of Panchayats in rural development and inclusive governance.

Eminent speakers including government officials and professors addressed the gathering, discussing the constitutional provisions of the 73rd Amendment and its real-world applications. Students actively participated in a poster-making contest and interactive discussions.

The program emphasized democratic decentralization, women's participation in governance, and effective decision-making at the grassroots level. The session concluded with a student oath to stay informed and engaged in civic responsibilities.
