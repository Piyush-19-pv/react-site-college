IPR Workshop

The Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) Workshop at Aggarwal College aimed to create awareness among students and faculty about the importance and legal aspects of protecting intellectual property.

Experts from legal and academic backgrounds discussed topics such as copyrights, patents, trademarks, and industrial designs. The workshop was highly interactive, encouraging participants to ask questions and engage in real-world case studies.

Participants gained valuable insights into how intellectual property can be used to safeguard innovation and creative work. The event also highlighted how IPR plays a crucial role in academic research and entrepreneurship.

The workshop concluded with a certificate distribution ceremony and group discussions on hypothetical IPR challenges.
