Vivekananda Jayanti Celebration

Aggarwal College celebrated Vivekananda Jayanti with great enthusiasm and devotion to mark the birth anniversary of Swami Vivekananda, a great spiritual leader and philosopher.

The program included enlightening speeches on his teachings, a special prayer session, and a motivational talk for students to follow his principles of self-confidence, discipline, and service to the nation.

Students presented quotes, poems, and a short play depicting the life of Swami Vivekananda. The session concluded with a pledge to carry forward his ideals in daily life.
