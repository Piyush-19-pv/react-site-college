Farewell – Management Department
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
The Management Department of Aggarwal College Ballabgarh organized a grand farewell event to bid goodbye to its final-year students with pride and warmth. The farewell was a memorable affair, blending nostalgia, achievement, and celebration in a single day.

The ceremony opened with a welcome address by junior students, followed by a delightful sequence of performances including motivational skits, management-themed parodies, and team-building games. Speeches from faculty members highlighted the academic and leadership achievements of the batch, along with personalized messages and career advice.

A key highlight of the event was the “Leadership Legacy” segment where seniors shared their learnings from internships, live projects, and case study competitions. Awards were presented for leadership, innovation, entrepreneurship, and teamwork. The Principal and HOD praised the students for upholding the values of discipline, creativity, and vision.

The farewell concluded with a video showcasing moments from seminars, business fests, presentations, and celebrations over the years. A group photo session and networking dinner marked the end of the event — but the beginning of a new journey for the graduates as future managers and leaders.
