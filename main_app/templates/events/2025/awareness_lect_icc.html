Awareness Lecture – ICC
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad, conducted an impactful Awareness Lecture under the aegis of the Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) to sensitize students and staff about gender equity and the importance of a safe academic environment. This initiative was aimed at informing participants about the legal frameworks surrounding workplace harassment, especially as outlined in the POSH Act (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) 2013.

The session was attended by a large number of students and faculty members. The expert speaker provided deep insight into the responsibilities of institutions in promoting dignity, safety, and equal opportunity for all genders. With real-life examples and open Q&A sessions, students were empowered to understand complaint procedures and prevention mechanisms.

Visual aids, videos, and interactive discussions made the session dynamic and inclusive. The college reiterated its commitment to zero tolerance towards harassment of any kind and promised a transparent, supportive process for addressing concerns.

ICC at Aggarwal College ensures that awareness is not just a one-time activity but a continuous process that empowers every individual. Students are encouraged to speak out, support peers, and contribute to a culture of respect and accountability.
