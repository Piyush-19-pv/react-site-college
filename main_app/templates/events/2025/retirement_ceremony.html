Retirement Ceremony
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh recently hosted a heartfelt Retirement Ceremony to honor the contributions of its esteemed faculty members stepping into a new chapter of life. The event was held in the college auditorium and was graced by the presence of faculty, students, and dignitaries.

The ceremony began with a floral welcome, followed by a classical performance symbolizing gratitude and tradition. Principal Sir delivered a touching speech highlighting the unwavering dedication of retiring staff and their impact on thousands of students over the years. Students presented farewell songs and poetry dedicated to their mentors.

Each retiring member shared personal anecdotes and reflections of their academic journey, evoking both smiles and tears. Tokens of appreciation, mementos, and citations were presented by the management.

The highlight of the event was a video presentation showcasing their memories in classrooms, events, and departmental gatherings. Colleagues gave heartfelt tributes, reiterating how their presence shaped the soul of the institution.
