Awareness on Helmets
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
In an effort to promote road safety among youth, Aggarwal College Ballabgarh organized a special awareness program on the importance of wearing helmets. With the rising number of two-wheeler accidents, the session aimed to highlight how a helmet can be the difference between life and death.

The event began with a demonstration by traffic officials showcasing the impact of collisions without helmets using animated models. Students were stunned to learn how even low-speed crashes could lead to fatal injuries when not wearing proper headgear.

Posters, banners, and skits by NSS and NCC volunteers emphasized key messages like "Use Helmet, Stay Alive" and "Head Safety is Life Safety". The program included a pledge-taking session, where students promised to never ride without a helmet.

A video campaign was also screened, featuring real-life stories of survivors and families affected due to negligence. The program concluded with distribution of free reflectors and stickers promoting helmet use.
