Career Program – Commerce Department
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
The Commerce Department of Aggarwal College Ballabgarh organized a special Career Guidance Program to help students explore opportunities in the dynamic world of business, finance, and economics. The session was aimed at bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world industry expectations.

Eminent speakers including CA professionals, bank managers, and startup founders were invited to share their career journeys. The students gained insights into fields like Chartered Accountancy, Company Secretaryship, MBA in Finance, Digital Marketing, and Entrepreneurship.

A dedicated panel discussion helped clarify doubts about competitive exams like CAT, NET, and UPSC Commerce-based options. The panel also discussed the latest trends in FinTech, stock markets, e-commerce logistics, and sustainability in business.

Students participated in mock interviews, resume building workshops, and aptitude test simulations. Brochures and handouts were distributed on emerging career areas and government schemes supporting young professionals.

The Principal appreciated the department’s initiative and emphasized the need for such programs to keep students future-ready. Overall, the event was a great success, offering clarity, inspiration, and confidence to the commerce students.
