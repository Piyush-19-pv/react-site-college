Nukkad Natak
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
The NSS Unit of Aggarwal College Ballabgarh organized a thought-provoking **Nukkad Natak** (street play) to raise awareness on social issues like gender equality, drug abuse, and cleanliness. The event was held in the college premises and at nearby marketplaces to reach a wider audience.

With powerful performances, student volunteers used drama and dialogue to deliver impactful messages that encouraged civic responsibility and social change. Their captivating expressions, traditional props, and live dialogues engaged passersby and received great appreciation.

The Nukkad Natak served as an excellent medium for spreading awareness in a relatable and memorable way. It showcased the talent of the youth while also emphasizing key societal concerns that demand attention and action.

The college principal and staff appreciated the creative efforts of the students and encouraged more such awareness initiatives in the future.
