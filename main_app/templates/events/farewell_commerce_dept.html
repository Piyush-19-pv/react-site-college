Farewell – Commerce Department
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
The Commerce Department of Aggarwal College Ballabgarh hosted a heartwarming farewell ceremony to bid adieu to the outgoing batch of final-year students. The farewell, held in the college auditorium, was filled with emotions, memories, and cheerful celebrations.

The juniors organized cultural programs, music and dance performances, and shared heartfelt speeches expressing gratitude and best wishes. Faculty members also addressed the students, sharing words of wisdom and reminiscing about the memorable moments spent together.

Awards were given for Best Student, Academic Excellence, and other achievements, followed by a photo session and refreshments. The college Principal congratulated the students on completing a crucial phase of life and encouraged them to strive for success in their future endeavors.

The farewell concluded with a group dance and video montage of memories, leaving everyone teary-eyed yet filled with joy. The Commerce Department once again proved its commitment to holistic development by organizing such a thoughtful event.
