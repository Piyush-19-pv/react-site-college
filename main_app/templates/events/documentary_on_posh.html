Documentary on POSH
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
In a step toward ensuring a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment, Aggarwal College Ballabgarh organized a **Documentary Screening on POSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment)** at the workplace. This initiative was led by the ICC (Internal Complaints Committee) under the guidance of college authorities.

The event focused on spreading awareness about gender sensitivity, rights of women at the workplace, and institutional mechanisms available to address grievances. The documentary highlighted real-life narratives, legal frameworks such as the POSH Act 2013, and the role of ICCs in educational and corporate settings.

Students and faculty members attended the screening in large numbers, followed by an open discussion on how colleges can foster a culture of respect, dignity, and zero-tolerance for harassment. The ICC also provided handouts and FAQs related to reporting mechanisms, confidentiality rights, and procedural fairness.

A key highlight of the event was a short awareness quiz conducted post-screening, helping attendees reflect on their understanding of the POSH law and ethics. Informational posters were displayed across the campus to reinforce key takeaways from the session.
