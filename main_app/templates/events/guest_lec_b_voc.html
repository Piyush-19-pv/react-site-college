Guest Lecture – B.Voc Program
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, Faridabad
Aggarwal College Ballabgarh organized an impactful Guest Lecture under the B.Voc (Bachelor of Vocation) program to bridge academic knowledge with industry practices. The lecture was delivered by industry experts and academicians who shared valuable insights on employability skills and vocational excellence.

The event aimed to expose students to real-world work environments and trends. Speakers discussed key areas such as technical skill development, workplace etiquette, communication skills, and career building in vocational streams. Topics covered also included entrepreneurship, financial literacy, and adapting to digital tools in modern workspaces.

Students participated in an interactive Q&A session, gaining a broader perspective of how their academic journey aligns with future job prospects. The lecture also emphasized the importance of internships and apprenticeships that form the backbone of B.Voc training.

The session concluded with a vote of thanks and a feedback collection round to improve future training sessions. Students appreciated the guest’s real-life experiences and were motivated to pursue skill-based excellence with greater enthusiasm.
