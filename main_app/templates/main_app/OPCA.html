Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC)

The Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC) is a web-based tool provided by Aggarwal College that enables students, faculty, and researchers to explore the college's vast library resources. Users can conveniently search books, journals, e-resources, and media content from anywhere with internet access. OPAC enhances transparency and efficiency in accessing academic materials, and helps users track availability, new arrivals, authors, publication details, and more—all in real time.
