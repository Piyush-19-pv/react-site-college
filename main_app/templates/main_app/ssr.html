Self Study Report (SSR)

The Self Study Report (SSR) is an essential component of the accreditation process by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council). At Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, we take pride in our commitment to continuous quality improvement and academic excellence. The SSR is a reflection of our internal assessment and evaluation of various aspects of institutional functioning, including academic programs, teaching-learning processes, governance, infrastructure, research, and community engagement.

Through this comprehensive report, we present our achievements, strengths, and areas identified for enhancement. The report highlights the proactive initiatives taken to meet the ever-evolving educational challenges and showcases the efforts made toward the holistic development of our students. Our SSR demonstrates how the institution aligns itself with the vision of transforming lives through education, innovation, and inclusive growth.

The preparation of the SSR involves a collaborative effort from all stakeholders, including faculty, administrative staff, students, alumni, and the management. It emphasizes our dedication to transparency, accountability, and strategic planning for sustained development. We invite all stakeholders and external evaluators to access and review our Self Study Report, which serves as a roadmap for our future aspirations and academic vision.
