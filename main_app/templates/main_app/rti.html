Statutory Declaration under Section 4(1)(b) of the RTI Act, 2005

Aggarwal College Ballabgarh makes a statutory declaration under Section 4(1)(b) of the RTI Act, 2005. This declaration is intended to promote transparency and provide easy access to information. The RTI Cell ensures that citizens can access public information as per the rules and provisions of the Act.
