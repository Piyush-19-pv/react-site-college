Chairman’s Message

I am delighted to welcome you all to another exciting academic year at Aggarwal College Ballabgarh... As we embark on this journey together, I want to extend my heartfelt greetings and share a few thoughts that I hope will inspire and guide us in the year ahead.

In the last five decades the college has obtained a name at the National and International Level. It has been awarded 'A++' grade with CGPA 3.57 by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council Bengaluru in 2019.

At Aggarwal College, we have always been committed to excellence in education, fostering an environment that encourages intellectual growth, innovation, and personal development. Our institution has a rich history of producing graduates who have gone on to make significant contributions to their fields, their communities, and the world at large.

As we move forward, our commitment to providing a world-class education remains unwavering. We are dedicated to continuously enhancing our curriculum, embracing emerging technologies, and adapting to the ever-evolving needs of our students. Our exceptional faculty members, staff, and administrators are here to support your academic journey and ensure that you have access to the resources and guidance necessary to succeed.

We encourage you to actively participate in the numerous clubs, organizations, and extracurricular activities that will enrich your college experience and help you grow as individuals.

In closing, I would like to extend my best wishes to each and every one of you for a successful and fulfilling academic year. Thank you for being part of the Aggarwal College family. Together, we will create a brighter and more promising future.

Best Wishes,
President, Governing Body
