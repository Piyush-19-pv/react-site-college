National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF)

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) is a methodology adopted by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, to rank higher educational institutions across the country. At Aggarwal College Ballabgarh, we believe in transparency, academic excellence, and continuous growth. We actively participate in the NIRF process to benchmark our academic and research performance against national standards.

Our college has consistently strived for excellence in teaching, innovation, outreach, and holistic development. By being a part of NIRF, we commit ourselves to providing world-class education and facilities. Our rankings and reports reflect the hard work of our staff, students, and stakeholders.
